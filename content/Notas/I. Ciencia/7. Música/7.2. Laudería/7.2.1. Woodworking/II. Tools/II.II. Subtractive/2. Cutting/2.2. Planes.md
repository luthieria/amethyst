# Types[^https://youtu.be/p27uJvUhZrU?si=CTkcnH4kC9EkVfmM][^https://woodworkersinstitute.com/hand-planes-an-introduction]
## 1. Bench
### 1.1. Smoothing (#3, #4)

- Use:
  - final smoothing.

![alt text](<Imagenes/Smoothing plane.png>)

#### #4
- The most common.
- Many furniture can be made just with it.
- → Great for beginners[^https://youtu.be/p27uJvUhZrU?si=CTkcnH4kC9EkVfmM][^Sellers].

### 1.2. Jack, Fore (#5, #6)

- **Jack** ≈ **fore**.
- "Fore" ← "before" ← was used before other planes.

<br>

- Medium-length.

<br>

- Versatile.
- Use:
  - initial rough work;
  - flattening;
  - (sometimes) smoothing.

![alt text](<Imagenes/Jack plane.png>)

#### #5
- The most popular and important Stanley plane.
  - Perfect as the first / only owned plane.
- #5 width = #4 width.

##### Setup
1. **Aggressive** (traditional):
   - mouth: wide;
   - blade: cambered heavily.
2. **Fine** (works as a smoothing plane):
   - mouth: narrow;
   - blade: cambered very lightly.
   - use:
     - jointing tasks on smaller boards (a jointer replacement);
     - shoot edges;
     - level table tops.

#### #6
- Metal-bodied is hard to use. Prefer the wooden-bodied #6.
- Can be used as a jointer.

#### 1.3. Jointer (#7, #8)
- Use:
  - jointing edges,
  - flattening large surfaces.

![alt text](<imagenes/Jointer plane.png>)

- **#7** = "**triplane**".
- **#8** = "**jointer plane**".
- Little difference between #7 and #8.

#### Stanley Sweetheart Low-Angle
- Use: 
  - levelling and jointing small things;
  - shooting;
  - smoothing (although too big).

## 2. Block
- Blade: 
  - lower angle,
  - bevel up.
- No chipbreaker.

<br>

- Can be held with one hand.

<br>

- Use:
  - across the grain on the end grain;
  - general purpose: 
    - taking down a knot, 
    - smoothing small pieces, 
    - chamfering edges.

# Order of importance (= acquisition)[^https://youtu.be/p27uJvUhZrU?si=CTkcnH4kC9EkVfmM]

1. **#4**.
   - [Lie Nielsen #4](https://www.lie-nielsen.com/products/no-4-smooth-plane) (cast manganese bronze) — M. E. Bruné’s favorite.[^https://youtu.be/vrob0sxibjg?si=v8fL21Qh_LK4dCSM]

   - [14” #5](https://www.lie-nielsen.com/products/no-5-jack-plane-?path=standard-bench-planes&node=4171) — necessary for guitar building.[^https://youtu.be/HqrJhw2wJog?si=uOpR9jZfCSuI9wL-]
