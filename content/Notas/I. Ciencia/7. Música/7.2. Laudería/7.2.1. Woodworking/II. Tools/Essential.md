# Necessary[^https://youtu.be/HqrJhw2wJog?si=uOpR9jZfCSuI9wL-]

- It’s possible to build a guitar with these only.

<br>

1. **Scalpel**.
   - M. E. Bruné prefers one without a handle for more control, but it’s not for everyone.
2. **Chisel**: [¾”](https://a.co/d/3HiiqNM).
3. [Bench **scraper**](https://a.co/d/j85i0XJ).
4. **Handplane**: #5, 14".
5. **File**: [standard milbuster](https://a.co/d/0ijshQ3c).
6. Fret **saw**.

# Quasi-Necessary[^https://youtu.be/EX52cyL9lbE?si=99dnKdtXGtr9_V-D]

1. [**Fan**](https://www.vevor.com/table-fans-c_13571/portable-desk-bladeless-fan-12-99-6-speed-quiet-table-fan-for-bedroom-office-p_010646855388?adp=gmc&srsltid=AfmBOopETHzx7vnDz5kdHOFgSNgod6eDJdFCLkNesMdCJUHkQCPTZBcplJQ&utm_content=YT3-CRtNxlJ9nXz5RtvpyLEnB6ECe9FB3FZX0Noh8BuWfkhGXnnZAtxAreMzeIIev00VIMg6UOr_QdFeuLDKrLqs5iA&utm_medium=product_shelf&utm_source=youtube) (~$14) — for better-laid and quicker finish.
2. **Sharpening station (stone)**.
   - The higher the grit, the better. Bruné uses [30 000](https://a.co/d/1Ijwv6y) (.49 microns). 
   - The finest leather is 1 micron. → After the stone, don’t sharp not even with leather.
3. **Center finder**.
4. [StewMac **Japanese Super Fine-Cut Saw**](https://www.stewmac.com/luthier-tools-and-supplies/types-of-tools/saws/japanese-super-fine-cut-saw?queryid=51500fb25c884253879872df939c664f) — cut off braces, rosetta tiles.
   - M. E. Bruné says sharpening it doesn't worth the time. → Buy a few.
5. **Carrier Infinity system** (climate control in the building), the one that keeps the 50% humidity perfectly the year round.
