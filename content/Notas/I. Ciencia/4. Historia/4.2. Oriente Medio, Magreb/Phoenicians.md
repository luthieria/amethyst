- **Phoenecians** = **Canaanites**.
- The 1st seagoing merchant empire.

<br>

- The language was mutually intelligible with that of the Israelites.
- In the time of King Solomon and later, Israelites worshipped the Phoenician gods Baal-Shamem (= Zeus), Baal-Melqart (= Herakles), Astarte (= Aphrodite).
