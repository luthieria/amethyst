- **Picts** — either: 
    - Celts;
    - Proto-Indo-Europeans;
    - ancient Europeans.
  - Probably Caledonians’ descendants.
- **Britons** (русский: **бритты**) — a Celtic people.
- **Caledonians** — either Britons / Picts.
- **Gaels** (русский: **гэлы** = **гаэлы**) = **Highlanders** (русский: **шотландские горцы**) —
  1. ancient people;
  2. modern ethnolinguistic group of Scots.

<br>

- Gaels + Picts = **Scots**.
- **Modern Scots**:
  1. **Highlanders** (Celts) — north, Gaelic language;
  2. **Lowlanders** (Germanics) — south, Scots language.
