- Бисмарк собрал Германию вокруг Пруссии, хотя она не была самой богатой или развитой из германских земель.[^https://youtu.be/46QV89uovRY]
- Bismarck:
  - built the colonial empire (1883–1884);
  - organized the Berlin Conference (1884–1885), which formalized the Scramble for Africa, intensified colonization (the tensions would eventually lead to WWI), eliminated most forms of African autonomy.
    - 1902: 90% of Africa colonized.
    - 1895: Liberia & Ethiopia — the only independent Sub-Saharan states.

<br>

1. **Herero and Nama genocide** (1904–1908) — the 1st genocide of the XX c.
   - Under colonial rule, natives were routinely used as slave laborers, and their lands stocked with cattle were frequently confiscated and given to colonists;
   - death from starvation and dehydration due to the prevention of the retreating Herero from leaving the Namib Desert by German forces;
   - once defeated, 1000s were imprisoned in concentration camps for slave labor;
     - the majority died of diseases, abuse, exhaustion;
     - medical experiments on prisoners;
   - deaths: 34 000–110 000.
2. **Maji Maji Rebellion** (1905–1907) — Tanzania.
   - The Great Hunger (njaa) followed, caused in large part by the German scorched-earth policy.
   - Destruction of villages, crops, and other food sources used by the rebels.
