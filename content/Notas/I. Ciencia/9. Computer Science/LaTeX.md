- Avoid spaces in file names.

---

- `\pagestyle{empty}` — no page numbers.

---
- `\\` — soft return (— no new paragraph).
- **\<empty line>** — hard return (— new paragraph).
---
- Math variables are italicized by default.
- `$...$` — math mode, inline.
- - `$$...$$` — math mode, display (= centered, on its own line).
- `{...}` — group.
  - E.g., `^{...}` — multiple-character superscript.
---
- `^` — superscript (only works in math mode).
- `_` — subscript (only works in math mode).
---
- `\ldots` — 3 dots ("...").
---
# Greek letters
- `\alpha` — α.
- `\pi` — π.
- `\Pi` — Π.
- Etc.

- E.g.: `$$A=\pi r^{2}$$` — \(A=\pi r^{2}\).
---
# Functions
- `$$y=\sin x$$` — \(y=\sin x\).
---
# 
- `\displaystyle` — display style in inline math mode (big font size).
